General Service Map

Create a map of your A.A. General Service area that you can embed on your website. The more areas that participate, the more complete the map will become.

To add the map to your website, add the following code to your HTML file: 

<script src="https://general-service-map.netlify.app/app.js" type="module" async></script>
<div id="map" data-area="3"></div>
