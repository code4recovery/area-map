Area Map

Create a map of your A.A. General Service area that you can embed on your website. The more areas that participate, the more complete the map becomes.

Add your district boundaries

The map gets its data from a crowdsourced list of Google My Maps links on the Area Maps Google Sheet. Check that sheet, someone might have started your area already. If you'd like to make edits, or if you would like to be listed as the contact because you already own the sheet, just leave a comment on the sheet.

To create a fresh map please follow these instructions.

Embed the map on your site

Once your area is added, you can embed the map on your website by adding the following code to your HTML. Replace the data-area attribute with the number of your area. 

<script src="https://area-map.netlify.app/app.js" type="module" async></script>
<div id="map" data-area="3"></div>
